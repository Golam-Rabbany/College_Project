Welcome to Dinajpur Polytechnic Institute

The government of Purbo Bangla established 16(sixteen) polytechnic institutes in each district of Purbo Bangla. Dinajpur polytechnic institute at south Balubar.i was one of them which is in a pleasant environment adorned with numerous plants, open and free from noise. Currently 49 polytechnic institutes have been established. This polytechnic was started with Civil and Power Technology in 1964. Later on Mechanical Technology in 1968, Electrical Technology in 1986, Computer Technology and Architecture and Interior design Technology in 2004 was started in this polytechnic institute. There are academic buildings, Workshop/Laboratory and the best educational facilities in this large institution of 21.4 acres of land. There are 240 students in the two Boy’s hostels and 100 students in a Girls hostel. They are getting all types of residential facilities by the side of study. There is a residential building in the campus for teachers and staffs. There are a commodious library enriched with 35658 books for earning knowledge, a mosque next to the entrance of the institution, a large auditorium for cultural studies and a large playground.

This institute was established in 1964 with the concept of producing product in to productivity. It is reliable and dependable.DPI aims at working closely with the industries to satisfy the needs of job market home and abroad. We provide higher order skilled manpower. We provide support to the passing graduates for placement into the decent job. We are committed to carryout the mission and visions of related agenda of the government. My best wishes to you all.
